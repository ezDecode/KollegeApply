Overview

Amity University is one of India’s largest private education groups with 11 universities, 25 schools, 28 global campuses, and over 250,000 alumni. More than 6,000 faculty members blend academic expertise with industry exposure, delivering learning through 400+ state-of-the-art labs, digital studios, and entrepreneurship zones. Students benefit from international exchange programmes, industry-funded research, and a curriculum co-designed with global partners.

The university’s innovation ecosystem is anchored by the Amity Innovation Incubator, which has supported 300+ start-ups, including several funded ventures. Scholarships worth ₹300 crores are awarded annually to meritorious and deserving students, making quality education accessible.
